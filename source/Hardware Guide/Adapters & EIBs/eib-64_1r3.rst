eib-64 v1.3
#########################

**eib-64 v1.3** is a 64-channel electrode interface board (EIB) with electrical and optical
stimulation. It is designed for small animal (e.g. mouse) tetrode electrophysiology and is compatible with :ref:`headstage_64_1r4` and is designed to be used with the mouse variant of the `shuttle-drive <https://open-ephys.org/shuttledrive>`_.
